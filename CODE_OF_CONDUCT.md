# Code of conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Harassment, discrimination, threats,
and deliberate disclosure of another person's private information are not
acceptable.

Security reports and credential-bearing material must never be posted publicly.
Use the private channel documented in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).

Project maintainers may edit or remove abusive, unsafe, off-topic, or
credential-bearing content and may restrict participation when necessary to keep
the project safe and productive.
